Mission

My mission is to apply the skills that I've developed to help create a more sustainable world. We only have one Earth and it's important to live with nature, not against it. And, as technology continues to develop, we can apply these advanced skills to better the world, which I'd like to be apart of. Traveling is also a major part of my life, and the more that I travel, the more I become aware of the common issues people face, such as not having power and clean water. In the future, I hope to work with teams to develop products that can make basic human rights more accessible to all.
